Why do we love cats?

They will sit in your lap but they don't need to be there. They communicate by purring and mewing. They're wild like birds but content to sit in your house. They live twice as long as bunnies. Unlike dogs they’re fine in the smallest apartment no yard necessary.

In short they combine the best of all pets: warm, cuddly and communicative but just wild enough to remind us of the magnificence of the animal kingdom that lies right outside our doors.
